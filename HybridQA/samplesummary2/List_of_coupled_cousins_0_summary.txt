Text: Prince Adalbert of Prussia ( Adalbert Ferdinand Berengar Viktor ; 14 July 1884 - 22 September 1948 ) was the third son of Wilhelm II , German Emperor by his first wife , Princess Augusta Victoria of Schleswig-Holstein .

Princess Adelaide Adi of Saxe-Meiningen ( Adelaide Erna Caroline Marie Elisabeth ; later Princess Adalbert of Prussia ; 16 August 1891 - 25 April 1971 ) was a daughter of Prince Frederick John of Saxe-Meiningen and his wife Countess Adelaide of Lippe-Biesterfeld .

Ernst Christian Carl , 4th Prince of Hohenlohe-Langenburg ( 7 May 1794 - 12 April 1860 ) . He was the son of Prince Charles Louis of Hohenlohe-Langenburg and Countess Amalie Henriette of Solms-Baruth .

Princess Feodora of Leiningen ( Anna Feodora Auguste Charlotte Wilhelmine ; 7 December 1807 - 23 September 1872 ) was the only daughter of Emich Carl , Prince of Leiningen ( 1763-1814 ) , and Princess Victoria of Saxe-Coburg-Saalfeld ( 1786-1861 ) . Feodora and her older brother Carl , 3rd Prince of Leiningen , were maternal half-siblings to Queen Victoria of the United Kingdom . She is a matrilineal ancestress of Carl XVI Gustaf of Sweden and of Felipe VI of Spain .

This is one of several instances of royal marriages between relatives, including the marriages of Adolphus Frederick V, Grand Duke of Mecklenburg-Strelitz and Adolf Frederick, King of Sweden. These marriages were often due to the limited pool of eligible royal partners.
